Services

We offer the following services

Dog Sitting

Your dog is cared for in a calm, safe, and loving environment where their routine comes first. Whether it’s a day, overnight, or longer stay, I provide attentive, home-style care with plenty of walks, play, and affection.

Dog & House Sitting

Enjoy peace of mind knowing your home and dog are both well looked after while you’re away. I stay in your home to maintain your dog’s normal routine, keep your property secure, and ensure everything runs smoothly.

Dog Walking

Regular, stimulating walks tailored to your dog’s energy level and needs, providing exercise, mental stimulation, and socialisation to keep your dog happy and healthy.

Dog Grooming

Gentle grooming services to keep your dog clean, comfortable, and looking their best. Each session is handled patiently and calmly, especially for nervous or sensitive dogs.

Dog Friendly Holidays

A relaxed, enrichment-focused stay with lots of outdoor time, exercise, and social interaction — creating a holiday experience your dog will love.

Dog Training

Positive, reward-based training tailored to your dog’s personality and your goals, building confidence, trust, and long-lasting results.
