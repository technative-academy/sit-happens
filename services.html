Services

We offer the following services

Dog Sitting

Your dog is cared for in a calm, safe, and loving environment where their routine comes first. Whether it’s a day, overnight, or longer stay, I provide attentive, home-style care with plenty of walks, play, and affection—so your dog feels relaxed and secure while you’re away.

Dog & House Sitting

Enjoy peace of mind knowing your home and dog are both well looked after while you’re away. I stay in your home to maintain your dog’s normal routine, keep your property secure, and ensure everything runs smoothly—so you return to a happy dog and a well-cared-for home.

Dog Walking

Regular, stimulating walks tailored to your dog’s energy level and needs. Whether it’s a steady stroll or an active adventure, walks provide exercise, mental stimulation, and socialisation, helping your dog stay healthy, happy, and well-balanced.

Dog Grooming

Gentle grooming services help keep your dog clean, comfortable, and looking their best. From brushing and bathing to basic coat and paw care, each session is handled patiently and calmly, making grooming a positive experience—especially for nervous or sensitive dogs.

Dog Friendly Holidays

Give your dog a holiday of their own while you’re away. Dog holidays offer a relaxed, enrichment-focused experience with lots of outdoor time, exercise, and social interaction, creating a fun and stress-free stay that feels more like a break than boarding.

Dog Training

Training is tailored to your dog’s personality and your goals, focusing on positive, reward-based methods that build trust and confidence. From basic obedience to improving behaviour at home or on walks, sessions are practical, supportive, and designed to create lasting results you can continue every day.
