About Us

We are a Brighton-based dogsitting and walking service.

We are dog people.

We offer a variety of walks covering a variety of walks by the seaside, woodland and local parks. We know socialisation and exercise are important to your dog so we provide varied walks to keep your dog active, happy and healthy.

We pride ourselves on our reliability, genuine care for your four-legged friends and our full range of dog walking services. These dog services include our 1 hr walks, weekend walks (for pet sittings), pet friendly taxi services, taxi service for vet appointments, pet feeding and our famous Friday Mega Hike. We promise to bring your pet home safe, tired and above all happy in our pet friendly vehicles. Get in touch now to see how our dog walking company can meet your animal needs.
